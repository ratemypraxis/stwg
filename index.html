Activity: No Archive Will Restore You

Design a profile on a past (data) self. Choose a specific time period (say half a year, 10 years ago) and find all information attached to your name or digital identity otherwise from that era, accurate or false. From those quantified perceptions, think about how you would approach such a person as framed.
